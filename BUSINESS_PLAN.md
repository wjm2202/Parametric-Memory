# mm-memory Business Plan

## 1. Market Research
- **Target Markets:**
  - AI/ML infrastructure (startups, research labs, enterprise AI teams)
  - Autonomous agent platforms (RPA, scientific research, knowledge management)
  - SaaS platforms needing explainable, auditable memory (finance, legal, healthcare)
  - LLM orchestration and agentic workflow tools
- **Market Trends:**
  - Growth in autonomous agents and LLM-based workflows
  - Demand for explainability, provenance, and durable memory in AI
  - Regulatory/compliance needs for auditable AI systems
- **Competitors:**
  - Vector DBs (Pinecone, Weaviate, Chroma)
  - Knowledge graphs (Neo4j, Stardog)
  - Proprietary agent memory (OpenAI, Anthropic, LangChain)
  - **mm-memory’s edge:** composable, explainable, auditable memory substrate with proof chains

## 2. Business Model & Go-to-Market
- **Business Models:**
  - Open-core (core open source, advanced/compliance/commercial support paid)
  - SaaS (managed mm-memory cloud)
  - Enterprise licensing (on-prem, compliance, support)
  - Consulting/services (integration, onboarding, custom dev)
- **Go-to-Market Steps:**
  1. Launch open-source repo with strong docs/community
  2. Publish benchmarks and case studies
  3. Integrate with agent frameworks (LangChain, AutoGen, RPA)
  4. Offer hosted SaaS with dashboards
  5. Target early adopters in research, AI startups, regulated industries

## 3. Intellectual Property Protection
- Copyright: code, docs, onboarding
- Patents: novel substrate/proof chain (consult IP counsel)
- Trademarks: brand, logo, product names
- Contributor agreements: CLA for all contributions
- Open source license: AGPL/SSPL/BSL (Affero GPL, Server Side Public License, or Business Source License) for commercial protection

## 4. Marketing & Sales Management
- **Marketing:**
  - Technical blogs, benchmarks, use cases
  - Community building, webinars, event sponsorship
  - Partnerships with frameworks/clouds
  - Thought leadership: whitepapers on explainable AI memory
- **Sales:**
  - Inbound: open-source adoption, content marketing
  - Outbound: enterprise AI, regulated industries
  - Customer success: onboarding, support, integration
  - Build reference customers and case studies
